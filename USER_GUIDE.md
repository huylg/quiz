# User Guide

## Join a Quiz

1. Open the app and enter the quiz ID.
2. Tap "Join Quiz".
3. Wait for the confirmation message.

If the ID is invalid, you will see an error and can retry.

## Answer Questions

1. Read the question and answer options.
2. Tap an answer and submit.
3. Wait for the score update after submission.

## Understand Your Score

- Your score updates after each answer.
- Correct answers add points, incorrect answers add zero.

## Read the Leaderboard

- The leaderboard shows ranks, names, and scores.
- Your row is highlighted for quick recognition.
- Ties are ordered deterministically.

## Troubleshooting

- Connection lost: the app will attempt to reconnect.
- Reconnect failed: return to join screen and rejoin.
- Slow updates: check your network connection.
